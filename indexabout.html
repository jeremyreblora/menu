"The Lewis College is an educational community and a Center of Excellence founded on the Christian Values of Faith, Service, and Personhood. It exists to provide the Bicol Region with high quality and low-cost education that is accessible to all students aspiring to be productive members of the society, propelling them as catalysts for progress in the region. The Lewis College is committed to: 1) Serving the people of God through a value-focused educational environment; 2) Developing entrepreneurial leadership and information technology competencies; 3) Forming students to be well-rounded, skilled, and professional men and women."
